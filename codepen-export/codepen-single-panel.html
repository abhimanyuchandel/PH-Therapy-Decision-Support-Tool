Clinical Decision Support Prototype

PH Therapy Navigator

Structured support for when to initiate or escalate pulmonary hypertension therapy, with score-driven recommendations and medication-level safety context.

Patient Inputs

Enter available data. The tool computes hemodynamics and risk strata, then proposes therapy options.

Risk Score Inputs (WHO Group 1 only)

Choose a risk model, then use quick-click categories or exact values. Available options change by assessment stage.

Clinical Policy Layer

This controls how the app places sotatercept/Winrevair within escalation pathways. The active policy version is shown below the application.

Current Group 1 PAH Regimen

Follow-up escalation only. Select ongoing therapies to tailor add-on or escalation recommendations.

Group 4 CTEPH / CTED Workflow

Use the ALA/PHA guidance-to-guidelines CTEPH pathway to clarify operability, BPA candidacy, and the post-PTE branch before surfacing riociguat.

Phenotype Modifiers

Use these to adjust pathway selection when phenotype is atypical or condition-specific.

Safety and Interaction Modifiers

These directly affect contraindication checks and medication filtering.

Embedded Guardrails

  • Blocks or flags guideline-discordant PAH therapy use in Group 2 and most Group 3 scenarios.
  • Flags high-risk contraindications (for example riociguat + PDE5 inhibitor; nitrates with PDE5/riociguat).
  • Highlights pregnancy-related restrictions for ERAs and riociguat.
  • Surfaces dose, route, titration, monitoring, and organ-function considerations for selected drugs.

For clinician decision support only. Final prescribing decisions must use current product labeling, local protocol, and specialist judgment.
